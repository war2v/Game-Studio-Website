Hunt Bounties, Explore solar systems, and more in Ruin Hunter

Venture into the unknown in Ruin Hunter, a high-octane first-person shooter set in the distant future where humanity’s curiosity leads to a deadly encounter with ancient alien civilizations. As an elite explorer and soldier, you are sent to distant planets to uncover the mysteries hidden within long-abandoned alien ruins. But what you find lurking in the shadows is far more dangerous than anyone imagined. Form fragile alliances, scavenge for scarce resources, and face the relentless hordes of the undead. But beware—it's not just the zombies you have to fear. In this unforgiving world, trust is a luxury, and the line between humanity and monstrosity blurs with every step you take.

Armed with cutting-edge weapons and advanced technology, you must navigate treacherous alien landscapes, solve intricate puzzles, and survive relentless enemies that guard the secrets of these ancient worlds. The ruins are filled with traps, hostile forces, and enigmatic relics that could either save humanity or doom it.

With fast-paced combat, breathtaking environments, and a storyline that will keep you on the edge of your seat, Ruin Hunter offers an intense and immersive FPS experience. Unravel the mysteries of the cosmos, face off against powerful alien adversaries, and discover the truth buried deep within the ruins—before it’s too late. Explore the unknown. Fight for survival. Uncover the secrets of the ruins.
