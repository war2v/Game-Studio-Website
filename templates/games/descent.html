Every Choice Matters...

In a world ravaged by a mysterious outbreak, society has collapsed, and the dead walk among the living. Descent plunges you into a gripping, story-driven zombie apocalypse where every decision you make could be your last. As one of the few remaining survivors, you must navigate a landscape filled with danger, desperation, and moral dilemmas. Form fragile alliances, scavenge for scarce resources, and face the relentless hordes of the undead. But beware—it's not just the zombies you have to fear. In this unforgiving world, trust is a luxury, and the line between humanity and monstrosity blurs with every step you take.

With a rich narrative, complex characters, and intense gameplay, Descent challenges you to survive the apocalypse, not just physically, but morally. Will you maintain your humanity in the face of overwhelming odds, or will the darkness consume you? The fate of the world—and your soul—rests in your hands.
